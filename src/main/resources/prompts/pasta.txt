Time: 15-25 min
Ingredients:
200 g dry pasta (spaghetti, penne)
1 tbsp olive oil
2 garlic cloves, sliced or minced
400 g canned tomatoes
Salt, pepper, pinch red pepper flakes
30 g grated Parmesan
Method:
Bring a large pot of water to a boil, salt well. Add pasta and cook to al dente per package; reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain.
While pasta cooks, heat oil in a skillet over medium. Add garlic and cook 40s until fragrant.
Add tomatoes and simmer 6 min to thicken; season with salt and pepper.
Add drained pasta to the sauce with 2 tbsp reserved pasta water; toss and cook a minute until sauce clings to the pasta.
Stir in Parmesan and herbs, adjust seasoning, and serve hot.
