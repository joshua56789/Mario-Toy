Time: 25-40 min
Ingredients:
200 g spaghetti
300 g ground beef
1 tbsp olive oil
1 small onion, finely chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
400 g canned tomatoes
Salt, pepper, pinch dried oregano or Italian herbs
Method:
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti al dente per package; reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain.
Heat oil in a skillet over medium. Saut√© onion until soft, add garlic 30-60s. Add ground meat, break up and brown; season with salt and pepper.
Stir in canned tomatoes. Simmer 10-15 min to thicken; add herbs and adjust seasoning.
Toss drained spaghetti into sauce; heat 1-2 min so sauce clings.
Serve with grated Parmesan and herbs.
