Time: 15-20 min, Oven: 200°C / 400°F
Ingredients:
1 baguette (250-300 g)
115 g (½ cup) softened unsalted butter
3-4 garlic cloves, minced (or 1½-2 tsp garlic paste)
2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped (or 1 tsp dried)
¼ tsp fine salt, pinch black pepper
Optional: 2 tbsp grated Parmesan, pinch red pepper flakes, lemon zest
Method:
Preheat oven to 200°C / 400°F.
Mix butter, garlic, parsley, salt and pepper (add optional extras if using).
Slice baguette 1-1.5 cm (½ in) diagonally, keeping base intact or cutting through
Spread garlic butter between slices and over top.
Wrap in foil for soft insides or leave uncovered for a crisp crust.
Bake covered 10-12 min, then uncover and bake 2-3 min to brown (or bake uncovered 10-12 min). Serve hot.
