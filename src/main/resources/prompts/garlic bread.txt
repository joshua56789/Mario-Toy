Time: 15-20 min
Ingredients:
1 baguette (250g)
115 g (0.5 cup) softened unsalted butter
3-4 garlic cloves, minced
2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
¼ tsp fine salt, pinch black pepper
Method:
Preheat oven to 200°C.
Mix butter, garlic, parsley, salt and pepper.
Slice baguette 1-1.5 cm diagonally, keeping base intact or cutting through.
Spread garlic butter between slices and over top.
Wrap in foil for soft insides or leave uncovered for a crisp crust.
Bake covered 10-12 min, then uncover and bake 2-3 min to brown. Serve hot.
