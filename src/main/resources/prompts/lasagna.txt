Time: 1 hr 15-90 min
Ingredients:
500 g ground beef
1 tbsp olive oil
1 small onion, finely chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
700 ml marinara sauce
9-12 lasagna sheets
250 g ricotta
1 egg
300-350 g shredded mozzarella
50g grated Parmesan
Method:
Preheat oven to 190°C.
Heat oil, sauté onion until soft, add garlic 30 sec, add ground meat and brown; season with salt and pepper.
Stir in marinara, simmer 8-10 min (thicken slightly). Add 60-120 ml water to sauce.
Mix ricotta with half the Parmesan, a pinch salt and herbs.
In a 9×13" dish: spread a thin layer of sauce, arrange 4 lasagna sheets, spread 1/3 ricotta, sprinkle mozzarella, pour sauce. Repeat 2 more layers. Finish with sauce, remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
Cover with foil (shiny side in) and bake 40 min; uncover and bake 10 min until bubbly and golden.
Let rest 10-15 min before slicing. Serve hot.
